About the Vampires

Vampires Motorcycle Club promotes motorcycling for our own amusement.

Founded in 1954, the club has chapters in Santa Cruz, San Francisco, and San Luis Obispo, California, with additional members throughout the United States and abroad.

Our membership is comprised of a diverse group of individuals who share one thing: a passion for motorcycles. We ride rain or shine, street, dirt, track, and adventure, from supermoto to supersport to superstupid.

Vampires MC does not discriminate on the basis of color, race, gender, orientation, make, model, or style of riding. If you are on two wheels, you are welcome.

We choose our family based on their character and zeal for riding.

Our rides and events are always free, and always open to all makes and models of both motos.

Events

We host a number of events throughout the year. All Vampires MC events are always absolutely free and open to all types of bikes and riders. If you’re on two wheels, you are welcome.
